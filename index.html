AfroBench: How Good are Large Language Models on African Languages?

ACL 2025
Multi-task benchmark for evaluating LLM performance across 64 languages, 17 tasks, and 23 datasets. Reveals systematic capability gaps in reasoning and knowledge tasks for low-resource settings.

IrokoBench: A New Benchmark for African Languages in the Age of Large Language Models Outstanding Paper · NAACL 2025

NAACL 2025
Evaluates whether LLMs can solve harder tasks beyond basic classification — covering NLI, MGSM, and MMLU-style benchmarks for African languages.
benchmarking evaluation reasoning

DIVERS-Bench: Evaluating Language Identification Across Domain Shifts and Code-Switching

WMDQS workshop, COLM 2025
Stress-tests language identification systems on noisy, informal, and code-switched inputs. Reveals severe performance degradation (often >30 F1 points) outside clean monolingual data.
evaluation robustness failure modes

Reassessing Speech Translation for Low-Resource Languages: Do LLMs Redefine the State-of-the-Art Against Cascaded Models?

MRL workshop, EMNLP 2025
Systematic comparison of audio-capable LLMs vs. cascaded ASR+MT systems. Proposed 2-stage ASR correction strategy reduced WER by 54% and achieved 5.8× BLEU improvements.
speech evaluation benchmarking

InkubaLM: A Small Language Model for Low-Resource African Languages

Preprint
Demonstrates that small, well-designed models can be more practical than large LLMs in cost-sensitive, multilingual production environments.
language models efficiency production
